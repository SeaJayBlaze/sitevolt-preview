Privacy Policy

Effective June 12, 2026

SiteVolt is a two-person studio. We collect as little as possible, and we don't do anything clever or creepy with what we do collect.

1. What we collect

  • Your email address, if you join our monthly email list.
  • Build information: business details, photos, copy, and preferences you share with us during a website build.
  • Payment details are collected and stored by Stripe, our payment processor, never by us. See Stripe's privacy policy.

2. What we do with it

  • Build and maintain your website.
  • Send the monthly email you signed up for: one short email, unsubscribe anytime with one click.
  • Remind you before any billing charge.

3. What we never do

  • Sell, rent, or share your information with anyone for marketing.
  • Run advertising trackers on this site. This site currently uses no analytics or tracking cookies at all.
  • Send you anything you didn't ask for.

4. Who else touches your data

Only the services needed to run things: Stripe (payments), our hosting provider (your website's server), and our email service (the monthly list). Each only receives what it needs to do its job.

5. Your choices

You can unsubscribe from emails at any time, ask us what we hold about you, or ask us to delete it, just email us. If you cancel your plan, we hand your site and content over to you and delete what we no longer need.

6. Changes

If this policy changes, the new version is posted here with a new effective date.

7. Contact

Privacy questions? Email cjleising@gmail.com.
